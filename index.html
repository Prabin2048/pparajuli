About Me

I am a theoretical physicist working on quantum information sciences and condensed matter physics. I am originally form Nepal. I have completed a Master's degree in physics from Tribhuvan University, Nepal in 2016. After that, I came to the University of California, Merced to persue Ph.D. degree in Physics. I have completed the ph.D. degree in May 2022. From June 2022 to September 2023, I worked at the Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory as a Postdoctoral researcher. Since Dec 2023 I have been working at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville as a Postdoctoral researcher.
